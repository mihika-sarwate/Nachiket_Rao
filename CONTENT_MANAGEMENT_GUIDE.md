# Content Management Quick Reference

## How to Edit Your Website

### Accessing the Admin Panel

1. Go to: `http://localhost:3000/studio` (local) or `https://yourdomain.com/studio` (production)
2. Log in with your Sanity account
3. You'll see the content editor

### Editing Existing Content

1. **Click** on the section you want to edit in the left sidebar
2. **Click** on the document to open it
3. **Make your changes** in the editor
4. **Click "Publish"** button (top right)
5. **Refresh your website** to see changes

### Adding New Services

### Adding New Myths & Facts

1. Click **"Myths & Facts"** in sidebar
2. Click **"+ Create"** button
3. Fill in:
   - **Myth**: The common misconception
   - **Fact**: The actual truth
4. Click **"Publish"**

### Editing the Welcome Popup

1. Click **"Website Settings"** in sidebar
2. Scroll to:
   - **Welcome Title**: Change the popup heading
   - **Welcome Content**: Edit the popup message
3. Click **"Publish"**

### Updating Contact Information

1. Click **"Contact Information"** in sidebar
2. Update:
   - **Email**
   - **Phone**
   - **Address**
3. Click **"Publish"**

### Uploading Images

1. Click the **image field**
2. Click **"Select"** or drag and drop
3. Choose image from your computer
4. Click **"Upload"**
5. Use the **hotspot** tool to adjust the focus point
6. Click **"Publish"**

**Image Tips:**
- Use high-quality images (at least 1000px wide)
- JPG for photos, PNG for graphics
- Keep file size under 2MB for best performance

### Rich Text Editing

When you see a rich text editor (for content areas):

- **Bold**: Select text and click **B**
- **Italic**: Select text and click *I*
- **Headings**: Click the paragraph icon and choose heading level
- **Lists**: Click the bullet or number icon
- **Links**: Select text and click the link icon

### Publishing vs. Drafts

- **Publish**: Makes content live on your website immediately
- **Save Draft**: Saves your work without publishing
- The green dot means published, yellow means draft changes

## Content Tips

### Writing Effective Service Descriptions

- Start with what problem it solves
- Explain your approach
- Mention who it's for
- Keep it concise (2-3 short paragraphs)

### Creating Compelling Package Names

- Use emotional benefits: "Peace of Mind Package"
- Or be descriptive: "8-Week Wellness Program"
- Highlight value: "Complete Care Package"

## Common Questions

**Q: How long until changes appear on the website?**
A: Immediately after publishing. Refresh your browser to see them.

**Q: Can I preview changes before publishing?**
A: Yes! Save as draft, then check your website. Drafts won't show to visitors.

**Q: What if I make a mistake?**
A: No worries! You can always edit and republish. Sanity also keeps version history.

**Q: Can multiple people edit at once?**
A: Yes, but you'll see a notification if someone else is editing the same document.

**Q: How do I change the website colors?**
A: Go to Website Settings â†’ Primary Color field.

**Q: Can I add videos?**
A: Not in the current setup, but you can embed YouTube links in rich text.

**Q: How many services/packages can I add?**
A: Unlimited! Add as many as you need.
